Speed Trivia was the first of a series of web-based games for the Brain & Bones engagement platform. It harnesses the popularity of casual gaming to allow companies and individuals to connect with their customers, followers, and employees. This was the premiere project I spearheaded during my time at Brain & Bones. The product was implemented as a fully interactive web-based game, featuring fast paced trivia gameplay and real-time leaderboards. It may use of modern web technology including a fully responsive PWA, and was playable on any web-connected device.
