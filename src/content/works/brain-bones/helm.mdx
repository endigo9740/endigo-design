The customer-facing frontend web application for the Brain & Bones platform, the Helm allowed B2B clients to manage trivia-based games and tournaments, as well as monitor usage and engagement within their organization. I directed all key initiatives of the project from concept to execution, including designing and implementing the frontend client and collaborating within the developer team. This included most developer operation tasks, Stripe integration as our payment gateway, and implementing quality assurance testing alongside our QA engineer.
