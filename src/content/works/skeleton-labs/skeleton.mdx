I proudly serve as both the creator and core maintainer for [Skeleton](https://github.com/skeletonlabs/skeleton) - a free and open source library for managing web-based design systems. It provides an optional, opt-in component library for frameworks such as Svelte and React. Skeleton integrates with Tailwind to provide a uniform design language and structured framework for controlling the look and feel of your product and user experience, allowing you and your team to more quickly build and manage frontend interfaces at scale.
