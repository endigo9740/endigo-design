Jackpot Rising was a tournament platform for all types of games - including hardcore, casual/mobile, and even real world arcade devices. The platform allowed users to compete against one another in progressive eSports-like tournaments for real money, prizes, and digital goods. During my three years at Jackpot Rising, I managed the company’s branding, UX, product design, and spearheaded several key projects and initiatives. This includes both digital and physical branding efforts for the company.
