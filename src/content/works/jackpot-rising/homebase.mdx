Homebase was the customer-facing app for the Jackpot Rising platform and the primary means for managing tournaments, leaderboards, prize fulfillment, user moderation, and more. As a progressive web app (PWA), it featured a full role-based permissions system for large corporations. I was the project lead and oversaw all design and frontend implementation for this initiative.
