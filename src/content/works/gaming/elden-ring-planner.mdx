I’m a long-time fan of FromSoft games (Dark Souls, Bloodborne, Sekiro, etc). Their latest entry, [Elden Ring](https://en.bandainamcoent.eu/elden-ring/elden-ring), is a sprawling action RPG that’s found massive success since release. As a side project, I created a simple open source web application that allows players to visually browse and plan their character’s armor, armaments, and more. The launch of this tool garnered the attention of several prominent members of the Souls content creator community. The tool was created using an early iteration of my UI library, Skeleton.
